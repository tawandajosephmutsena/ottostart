The Challenge

To create a seamless digital experience that bridges the gap between physical retail and e-commerce, requiring a complete overhaul of the existing legacy infrastructure while maintaining brand integrity. The goal was not just to inform, but to immerse the user in the narrative of the product line, making the digital storefront feel as tangible as walking into a flagship store.

Interface Concept v2.0

The Solution

We implemented a headless architecture allowing for rapid content deployment across regions. Visually, we leaned into a 'Digital Brutalism' aesthetic—bold typography, high contrast, and raw layout structures—softened by fluid WebGL micro-interactions.

The core of the experience is a scroll-hijacked narrative that guides users through the product's history before presenting the purchase opportunity, increasing emotional investment.
