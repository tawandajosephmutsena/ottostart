01

What is your typical project timeline?

expand_more

Our timelines are bespoke to the project scope. Typically, a full branding and web engagement spans 8-12 weeks. We prioritize quality and depth over speed, ensuring every pixel serves a purpose. We work in 2-week sprints with transparent deliverables at each stage.

02

Do you work with early-stage startups?

expand_more

Yes, we love working with visionaries. For early-stage startups, we offer a "Launchpad" package that condenses our core branding and digital product services into a high-impact 4-week sprint, designed to get you to market or your next funding round with a world-class identity.

03

How is your payment structure defined?

expand_more

We operate on a 50/50 or 40/30/30 payment schedule for most projects. For larger, long-term engagements or retainers, we can structure monthly billing cycles. We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and crypto (USDC/ETH).

04

Do you offer post-launch support?

expand_more

Absolutely. A digital product is a living entity. We offer several retainer tiers for post-launch maintenance, analytics reviews, and iterative design improvements to ensure your platform scales with your user base.

05

Can you handle just the development?

expand_more

We prefer to handle both design and development to ensure the highest fidelity. However, for select partners with strong internal design teams, we do offer development-only services focused on WebGL, Creative Coding, and complex Front-end implementation.
